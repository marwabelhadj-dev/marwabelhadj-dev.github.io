Do Entrepreneurship Influencers Matter? 🏅 2nd Prize — SURC 2025

Co-authored study on entrepreneurial intentions among 272 Moroccan university students, applying the Theory of Planned Behavior and Social Learning Theory. Key finding: attitude toward entrepreneurship, subjective norms, and inspiring role models are the strongest predictors of intent — while social media influencers show limited direct impact. Analyzed via PLS-SEM using SmartPLS.
